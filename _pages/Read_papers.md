# Drosophila Proventriculus Anatomy

1. *[The Drosophila proventriculus lacks stem cells but compensates for age-related cell loss via endoreplication-mediated cell growth](https://www-nature-com.proxy1.library.jhu.edu/articles/s41467-026-68876-5)* (Ewen-Campen, 2026). This paper shows that the PV4 cells do not contain a stem-cell niche, but rather consist of endocycling cells that increase their ploidy as the fly ages to keep up wiht increased metabolic demands from PM synthesis. I mainly like this paper for the identification of new driver lines for the interior of the PV.
